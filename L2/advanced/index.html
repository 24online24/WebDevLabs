Our Journey

From cart to café and beyond

2018

Started as a one-person espresso cart on SW 3rd Avenue with a dream and a single-group La Marzocca.

2019

Opened our first brick-and-mortar shop on Maple Street. Hired our first barista and baker.

2021

Launched our in-house roastery — sourcing directly from farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala.

2023

Won Portland's Best Coffee Shop award. Expanded seating with a cozy outdoor patio.

2025

Introduced our subscription box — single-origin beans shipped fresh to your door every month.
