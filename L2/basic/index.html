About Us

Bean & Brew was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: bring exceptional coffee to our neighborhood. We source our beans from family-owned farms and roast them right here in our shop.

Whether you're here to work, catch up with friends, or just enjoy a quiet cup, you'll always find a seat and a smile waiting for you.
