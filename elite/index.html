Our Journey

From cart to café and beyond

2018

Started as a one-person espresso cart on SW 3rd Avenue.

2019

Opened our first brick-and-mortar shop on Maple Street.

2021

Launched our in-house roastery — direct farm sourcing.

2023

Won Portland's Best Coffee Shop. Expanded with a patio.

2025

Subscription box launched — fresh beans delivered monthly.
